  <h2 class="uvIdeaTitle"><a href="/forums/304921-excel-for-windows-desktop-application/suggestions/8620705-add-cell-spacing-buffers">Add cell spacing/buffers</a></h2>
    <div class="uvIdeaDescription uvIdeaDescription-truncated">
